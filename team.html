Welcome to ModelLabs


Vision
We have changed manifolds in all our domains of physics over the past 100 years. Our society has transformed from Newton's Laws to Einsteins' special theory of relativity, but our labs have not transformed at such an advancing pace. Our high school labs are still teaching the same old experiments, we here at Model Labs have envisioned the future of every lab. We are working tirelessly to achieve our goal of transforming the labs by introducing new and transformed experiments that are relevant in this modern era and contemporary world.

About Us


An initiative guided by esteemed professor emeritus H. C. Verma of IIT Kanpur and made reality by the enthusiastic students of the esteemed institute. Our goal is to advance the Indian education system by coming up with a refined model for science laboratories which is relevant to contemporary scientific advancements, and we are focusing on the high school physics labs as of now and aim to extend it further.
